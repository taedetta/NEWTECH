Common Questions

Do I need any experience?

None at all. Discovery flights are designed for complete beginners — no prior knowledge required.

Will I actually fly the plane?

Yes! Your instructor will handle takeoff and landing, but you'll fly the aircraft in cruise flight.

How long does it take?

About 30 minutes in the air, plus a pre-flight briefing and debrief — plan for about 1.5 hours total.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes. No special gear needed.
